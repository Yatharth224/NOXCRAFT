METHODOLOGY

Our Process

A meticulously structured approach ensuring every project moves seamlessly from conceptualization to breathtaking reality.

01
troubleshoot

SWOT Analysis

Evaluating project Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats to establish a robust strategic foundation and mitigate risks early.

02
architecture

Design Dev.

Includes two stages: concept layout, defining the vision and aesthetics, and detailed design, refining layouts and materials.

03
request_quote

Cost Estimation

Forecasts project expenses, including materials, labor, and design fees. It ensures careful budgeting to prevent overspending.

04
category

Detailed Design

Detailed designing along with required material. A good for construction (GFC) drawing set, and 3D visualization.

05
inventory_2

Sourcing

Involves identifying, evaluating, and selecting suppliers or service providers that meet the project's precise material requirements.

06
account_tree

Co-ordination

Entails planning, organizing, and directing resources to ensure the project is completed on time, within budget, and to quality standards.

07
handshake

Delivery & Completion

Includes overall handover of the project, finalizing all tasks, transferring ownership to the client, and ensuring all deliverables are met precisely to spec.
