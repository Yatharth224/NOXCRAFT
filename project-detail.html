THE OBSIDIAN VILLA

COMPLETION

2023

TOTAL AREA

1,450 SQM

Structural Elegance

LOCATION

Lake Lugano, Swiss Alps

COLLABORATORS

Sarto Engineering, Atelier Noire

TYPOLOGY

Private Residential Luxury

Perched above the crystalline waters of Lake Lugano, The Obsidian Villa represents a dialogue between geometric severity and the organic fluidity of the alpine landscape. The project challenged our studio to create a dwelling that maximizes panoramic transparency while maintaining absolute acoustic and visual privacy.

The structural core utilizes a cantilevered steel frame finished in matte-black powder coating, supporting expansive triple-glazed panels that disappear into the floor. The interior finishes—honed Nero Marquina marble and hand-charred Yakisugi cedar—create a tactile sanctuary that shifts its character with the solar cycle.
